Achievement 2

“ Snehalaya is one of the gifted centre, designed to provide appropriate services for children identified as gifted.”

Snehalaya caters to the needs of special children achieving independence in activities of daily life, communication, education pre-vocational training & vocational activity according to the needs & abilities of the child. Art, music, movements, sports, recreation etc,

I encourage you to take advantage of the information on our site. I look forward to working with all of you to provide a student with the opportunities, guidance & support necessary to succeed, I value your input & welcome hearing from you at any time. It is over - whelming that we count more than 35 years from the inception of the school, what a journey it has been with each year having its own triumphs & struggles, we grew from 5 students to 60 & 2 staff members to 12 now, & still counting.

Achievement 3

“Snehalaya caters to the needs of special children achieving independence in activities of daily life,”

We take great pride to thank ITI Education Committee who have provided us well planned in house classrooms featured with good ventilation, toilets, pleasing architectural amenities , fans, lightings , fixtures , colourful paintings, & marble flooring etc.

We have developed well equipped, computer, audio visual & occupational therapy room, which will be a great help in the overall development of students. I am grateful to ITI Education Committee & Members for their selfless dedication & contribution to the welfare of our students at Snehalaya.
