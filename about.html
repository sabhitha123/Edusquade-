SNEHALAYA ITI School For Special Education & Vocational training centre for intellectually challenged children was established in the year 1985, at Vidya Mandir Premises, ITI Township, Dooravaninagar, Bangalore – 560016. Snehalaya is one of the gifted centre, designed to provide appropriate services for children identified as gifted. Each programme provides instructions at an accelerated pace that is up to 2 years above grade level. Students are encouraged to utilize critical thinking, problem solving & logical thinking. It is very well known that Snehalaya caters to the needs of children with intellectually disabled aged 5 to 25 years.
